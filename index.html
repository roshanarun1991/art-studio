About Roshan

As a Stockholm based artist and engineer, I try to find inspiration in everything around me. My art moves between abstract energy and figurative stories.

From finding the balance between colours to building thought-provoking ideas, my ambition is to explore the beautiful contrast between harmony and chaos.

Art is a therapeutic, joy-driven practice for me and most profits go to charities. If you have a cause close to your heart, I would love to create something that supports it.

Every piece is one-of-a-kind and comes with a certificate of authenticity. Feel free to reach out if something here speaks to you.
